## Overview

**Vidya Rakshak** is an immersive **metaverse-based education system built in Decentraland**, focused on **Environmental Studies** for students. It transforms traditional learning into interactive virtual experiences using 3D classrooms, simulations, and avatar-based participation.

Designed to promote **safe, focused, and engaging learning**, Vidya Rakshak enables students to explore environmental concepts through real-time interaction, virtual field visits, and collaborative activities in a decentralized digital campus.

## Features

- **Immersive Learning:** 3D virtual classrooms for better understanding of environmental concepts.  
- **Interactive Simulations:** Learn through virtual ecosystems, climate models, and real-world scenarios.  
- **Collaborative Spaces:** Group discussions and activities with students and mentors.  
- **Decentralized Platform:** Built inside Decentraland for open and future-ready education.  
- **Student-Centric Design:** Encourages engagement, exploration, and active learning.  
- **Future-Oriented Education:** Prepares students for digital and environmental awareness.
